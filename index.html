Jackalopes Are Awesome!

"Jackalopes are my spirit animal, and they should be yours too."
– Sia

They are a fearsome critter that looks like a jackrabbit with deer horns, but it's totally a real animal. For safety purposes, hunters have to wear stovepipes on their legs.

Diet

One way to catch a jackalope is to entice it with beignets, its top food of choice. The jackalope's second favorite food is collard greens. They like to drink semi-melted sno-balls, preferably satsuma limeade-flavored with the condensed milk add-on.
